Admissions

We welcome all families interested in becoming part of our vibrant school community. At Winchester Ridge Primary School, we are committed to making the admissions process clear, friendly, and supportive from start to finish.

How to Apply

  • Step 1: Download and complete the enrollment form below.
  • Step 2: Submit the form along with the following documents:
    • Your child’s birth certificate
    • Proof of address (e.g. utility bill or lease agreement)
    • Latest school report (if applicable)
    • Immunization card
  • Step 3: Schedule a welcome visit and meet with our staff and principal.
  • Step 4: Await your confirmation letter with details about your child’s start date, class placement, and orientation.

Important Information

Admissions are open throughout the year, depending on class availability. We encourage families to apply early to secure placement, especially for Grades R and 1, which tend to fill up quickly.

We do not discriminate based on race, religion, or background — every child is valued and welcomed. Our admissions team is here to help you every step of the way.

If you have any questions or need help with the process, please reach out to our admissions team via the Contact page.
